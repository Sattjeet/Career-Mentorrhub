About Career Mentorrhub

At Career MentorrHub, we are dedicated to empowering students and parents with the right tools, insights, and support to confidently navigate their unique career journeys. We are here to simplify the often-overwhelming world of career choices and turn it into an inspiring journey of self-discovery and direction. Our approach is grounded in the powerful Japanese concept of "Ikigai" — the sweet spot where:

    • What you love,
    • What you are good at,
    • What the world needs, and
    • What you can be rewarded for, beautifully intersect to define a purposeful and fulfilling career.

Our Vission

To empower every student to discover their true potential, align their passions with purpose, and confidently pursue a fulfilling career that contributes to their personal growth and the betterment of society.

Our Mission

We aim to guide students and parents through a structured journey from self-awareness to career realization by providing step-by-step career guidance tailored to individual aspirations and aligned with emerging opportunities. Supporting families in building actionable, informed career roadmaps that foster confidence and purpose.

About The Counsellor

With over 28 years of extensive experience across schools, colleges, universities, and the corporate sector, our lead counsellor brings deep insight and expertise in the field of education, academic leadership, and career guidance. A strong advocate for student-centric development, she has successfully guided countless students through stream selection, college admissions, documentation, and decision-making for both academic and career pathways.

She has also worked extensively with students facing learning disabilities, offering compassionate guidance and collaborating with parents to build inclusive, supportive strategies that ensure every child gets the opportunity to thrive.

In recognition of her contribution to educational CSR, the project was awarded the Literary Hero Award by the Rotary Club for initiating a meaningful rural library project that brought learning resources to underprivileged communities.

Her commitment to awareness and inclusion is evident in her active participation in events such as the Walk for Dyslexia, organized by the Maharashtra Dyslexia Association, further affirming her dedication to advocating for students with special learning needs.

As a seasoned trainer and facilitator, she has led professional development programs for teachers and educational professionals, with a strong focus on areas such as documentation, management skills, marketing for educators, professional growth, and effective search strategies. Her dynamic sessions are known for being insightful, interactive, and practical.

In a recent milestone, she was invited by the Naval Commander at NCHC (P) to conduct a career awareness session titled "Unlock Your Career Potential" for the residents of the Naval Civilian Housing Colony. The event was well-received, offering valuable takeaways to both students and parents, and was appreciated for its clarity, relevance, and impact.
